MoodMix — Music That Understands You

A mood‑driven music experience built to help you reflect, express, and level up emotionally.

MoodMix blends emotional awareness with music discovery. Choose your mood, explore curated soundscapes, and track your emotional journey over time. Built with empathy, clarity, and user safety at the core.

Download MoodMix 1.5 The Story

How to Install (Android First-Time Guide):

  1. Download the moodmix1.5.apk file.
  2. Open the downloaded file from your notifications or 'Downloads' folder.
  3. If you see an "Unknown apps" prompt, tap Settings and toggle "Allow from this source".
  4. Go back, tap Install, and start your journey!
