Why I Built This App

I built MoodMix for the people who struggle to express themselves out loud — not because they don’t feel deeply, but because sometimes the words just don’t come out right. Maybe you’re frustrated, and it feels like no one really understands you but you. Maybe you need to write things out first to gain the confidence to speak them into existence. Maybe you’re carrying anger, hurt, or stress that you don’t want anyone else to see. MoodMix is for all of that and more.

It’s a space where you can be honest without judgment, where you can release what you’re holding, and where music helps say the things you can’t always say yourself. This app is rooted in therapeutic advice I received from my own therapist about the power of words and journaling, and the truth that music played a vital role in saving my life during my darkest moments.

What started as a project during my Mobile Application Design & Development class while pursuing my Bachelor of Science in Information Technology became something much more personal and real. I wanted to build something pure and honest for the heart, soul, and mind—not for money or thanks, but because I am tired of seeing the beauty of this life being eroded. This is a tool to help people process their emotions, reflect safely, and find their own space to breathe, grow, and heal.

Level Up Through Creation

While MoodMix is my personal contribution to mental clarity, I am also building a community space to highlight other creators, innovators, and thinkers who are helping the world "level up."
