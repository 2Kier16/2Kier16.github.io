Why I Built This App

I built MoodMix for the people who struggle to express themselves out loud — not because they don’t feel deeply, but because sometimes the words just don’t come out right. Maybe you’re frustrated, and it feels like no one really understands you but you. Maybe you need to write things out first to gain the confidence to speak them into existence. Maybe you’re carrying anger, hurt, or stress that you don’t want anyone else to see. Or maybe you had to stay professional in a moment when someone absolutely deserved the verbal lashing you couldn’t give them. MoodMix is for all of that and more.

It’s a space where you can be honest without judgment, where you can release what you’re holding, and where music helps say the things you can’t always say yourself or others. This app is rooted in therapeutic advice I received from my own therapist about the power of words, journaling, and the truth that music has also played a role in saving my life when I was ready to give up.

What started as a project during my Mobile Application Design & Development class while pursuing my Bachelor’s degree in Information Technology became something much more personal and real. I wanted to build something pure and honest for the heart, soul, and mind to truly help people; not for money or thanks, just because I'm tired of feeling my heart breaking, my head hurting, my soul feeling weak, and frustrated that THESE PARASITES are destroy everything beautiful in this life we are blessed to live in this or any moment. Something that I believe can help people process their emotions, reflect safely, and find their own space to breathe, grow, and heal. In order to deal with the mess this world is in.
